Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 4 cups marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until the meat is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the meat mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, basil, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  10. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  14. Enjoy your delicious lasagna meal!
