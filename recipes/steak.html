Steak

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs
  • Fresh thyme sprigs

Steps

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes before cooking.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Pat the steaks dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat until it begins to smoke.
  5. Add the steaks to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until a golden-brown crust forms.
  6. Add the minced garlic, butter, rosemary, and thyme to the skillet.
  7. Spoon the melted butter over the steaks as they cook for another minute.
  8. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or longer if desired.
  9. Remove the skillet from the oven and transfer the steaks to a cutting board. Let them rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing.
  10. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve with your favorite side dishes.
  11. Enjoy your delicious steak meal!
